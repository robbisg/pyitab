Representational Similarity Analysis
====================================
This analysis aims at finding common representation of stimulus (or experimental variable),
in different brain areas and across-modalities.

You can cite `Kriegeskorte et al., 2008 
<https://doi.org/10.3389/neuro.06.004.2008>`_
